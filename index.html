Everything done

  • Detailed Discussions: Thorough exploration of your business goals, target audience, and functional requirements through extensive consultations.


  • Initial Design Concepts: Creation and presentation of initial design mockups and concepts that align with your brand identity and preferences.


  • Approval Process: Collaborative review and refinement of design proposals to ensure they meet your expectations and requirements before moving to development.
  • Building Website Features: Implementation of essential features such as navigation structure, content layout, interactive elements, and backend functionalities.


  • Testing Functionality: Rigorous testing to ensure all website features work seamlessly across different devices and browsers, enhancing user experience.


  • Content Integration: Integration of optimized content, ensuring it aligns with SEO best practices and enhances user engagement.
  • Final Testing: Comprehensive testing of the entire website to identify and resolve any remaining issues or bugs before launch.


  • SEO Setup: Implementation of on-page SEO elements, including meta tags, keywords, and site maps, to improve search engine visibility.


  • Content Migration: Seamless transfer of existing content to the new website, ensuring continuity and consistency for your audience.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Regular monitoring of website performance, security, and uptime to address any issues promptly and ensure smooth operation.


  • Maintenance Tasks: Routine updates to plugins, themes, and security measures to protect against vulnerabilities and maintain optimal functionality.


  • Support Services: Responsive customer support and assistance with content updates, technical queries, and any additional requirements as per agreed terms.
  • Final Review: Conducting a comprehensive review to ensure all project deliverables meet agreed-upon specifications and quality standards.


  • Client Handover: Formal handover of the completed website, along with necessary documentation and training to empower clients in managing their site.


  • Feedback and Evaluation: Soliciting feedback from the client to assess satisfaction levels and identify areas for future improvement or enhancement.
